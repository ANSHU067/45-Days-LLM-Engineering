# Career Launchpad · Day 3 — Personal Brand ≠ Influencer

Day 3 of the daily career track. Removes the "I don't want to be an influencer" block and gets every
student to **commit to one primary platform** before any posting starts.

> 📁 This folder currently holds only the **career talk**. The Day-3 *code* (Booleans &
> conditionals) will be added to this same `Day-03-…` folder later.

## How to present it
Self-contained `index.html`. Double-click → **F** for fullscreen → **→ / Space** next, **←** back.

## Slide flow + speaker notes
10 slides, ~28–30 min.

| # | Slide | Talk about / ask the room |
|--:|-------|---------------------------|
| 1 | Title | "Today we make your reputation intentional." |
| 2 | The scary word | Ask: "Who feels weird about 'putting themselves out there'?" Normalize it. |
| 3 | Real definition | No-brand vs brand cards. Brand = known for something useful. |
| 4 | Build/learn in public | The core habit. Examples of low-bar posts anyone can make. |
| 5 | Reframe | Doctor's-nameplate analogy. It's information, not bragging. |
| 6 | Platforms | Be honest about effort vs payoff for each. |
| 7 | Match to goal | ⭐ The decision slide. India + job → LinkedIn. Push them to choose. |
| 8 | Minimum setup | One feed + GitHub. Lowers the perceived workload. |
| 9 | **Career Action** | Have them open the app and confirm the account in-session. |
| 10 | Close | Tease Day 4 (profile test). |

## Today's Career Action
**Pick your one primary platform** and confirm you have an account. Write down *why* (your goal).
